Our foundation

Rooted in
Ahimsa

Ahimsa is the ancient Sanskrit principle of non-violence. It is the single absolute principle of Earthity. Every decision we make passes one question: does this cause harm to any living being?

Not a religion. Not an ideology. A living ethics of action.
